DESIGN DYNAMO CHALLANGE

Description : The Design Dynamo Challenge is an exhilarating design competition where teams face the unexpected "Mystery Roulette." After receiving a prototype developed in Round 1, teams must swiftly adapt to a randomly assigned twist. The challenge demands lightning-speed creativity, adaptability, and design ingenuity.

Rules & Regulations :
  • Team will be select based on the round 1 (through online submission) the team can prepare the prototype based on their own theme.
  • Teams must use the prototype provided during Round 1 as a foundation for round 2.
  • The assigned twist from the "Mystery Roulette" must be integrated seamlessly into the prototype.
  • Presentations should highlight the adaptability to unexpected constraints.
  • Plagiarism is strictly prohibited.
  • The team will get 1hour 15 minutes for the creating the randomly assigned twist.
  • The teams will get 4 minutes for presentation, and 3 minutes for question and answer session.
    • Round 1- Prototype Development (online mode):

  • Team Formation : Teams develop an initial prototype based on their own theme. top prototypes move on to the next round.

  • Submission ,Evaluation and Shortlisting : Teams will design and submit their UI Figma prototypes through the designated online platform within the specified deadline. The submitted prototypes based on criteria such as user experience, interface design, creativity, and functionality. The top teams will be shortlisted to advance to the second round.
    • Round 2-Mystery Roulette Challenge (offline mode):

  • Mystery Roulette Setup: Teams will face a Mystery Roulette wheel which will be set up. Each section of the wheel will contain a unique twist or challenge.

  • Twists and Challenges: Upon spinning the wheel, teams will be presented with a twist or challenge, ranging from unforeseen user scenarios, technological constraints, design constraints, or thematic variations.

  • Adaptation and Prototyping: Teams must quickly adapt to the given twist and modify their UI Figma prototypes from Round 1 to incorporate the new requirements or constraints within the stipulated time frame.
  • Presentation to Jury: Once the adaptation phase concludes, teams will present their updated prototypes to the jury panel. Judges will evaluate how effectively teams have integrated the twist, demonstrated creativity, addressed user needs, and maintained design coherence.
  • Winner Determination: Winner Determination: Jury selects the winning team based on overall performance, adaptability, and alignment with challengeÂ objectives.
