TECHSCAPE ROOM

Description : Get ready for an exhilarating adventure! In this engaging challenge Team of max of 3 students will dive into the mystery room of challenges . Laptops and notepads are provided at each door, Teams have just 15 minutes to crack the codes and progress through the doors. The clock is ticking, adding a rush of excitement to this immersive and intellectually stimulating activity. The team with the shortest overall time will be emerged as the triumphant winner of this epic code-breaking challenge

Detailed description of the Event:
  • In this event teams comprising of 3 students will embark on an adventure through a room containing five rounds, As they enter, computers and notepads are provided .The teams objective is to unravel the code in each round with successful solutions allowing them to progress further into the next round.

  • CHALLENGE 1: Decode a program with syntax errors on a computer screen. Identify and fix the errors, then run the program to reveal the output.

  • CHALLENGE 2: Confront a challenge on the screen, sketch out the algorithm on the provided notepad. Let your creativity flow as you solve the problem.

  • CHALLENGE 3: Face an easy coding problem on the screen. Put fingers to the keyboard and write the solution on your laptop to move forward.

  • CHALLENGE 4: Take on a slightly harder coding problem. This is the pinnacle – solve it to prove your team's coding prowess and advance to victory!

  • The clock is ticking, as the duration of each team spends navigating the challenges is recorded. With a strict time limit of 15 minutes per team, the pressure is on to decode the mysteries within the allotted time frame.Two teams will compete with each other .While Upon completion, the team with the minimum time taken will progress to the finale round adding an element of competition and excitement to this immersive and intellectually stimulating activity.The same procedure will be followed for the second round.

    • Duration: 2 hours
    No of rounds: 2
    Round-Details :
  • Participants should not bring any external tools or devices into the escape room.
  • Handle all props and equipment carefully. Rough handling may result in disqualification.
  • Teams must carefully follow the provided instructions.
  • After reaching 15 minutes, the team must promptly exit the escape room.
  • Participants must patiently wait in the designated waiting area until it is their turn to enter the escape room.
  • Participants should have knowledge of programming languages such as C, C++, and Python.
