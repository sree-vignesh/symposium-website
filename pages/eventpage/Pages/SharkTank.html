SHARK-TANK

Description : Welcome to our 'Shark Tank' event, where innovation and entrepreneurship take center stage in our college community.This event is all about showcasing the brilliance of our student entrepreneurs.

Rules & Regulations :
Round-1(Online-Submission) :
  • Each team can contain 1 to 4 members.
  • Teams must submit a abstract and a PowerPoint presentation detailing their business idea via Google form
  • The Power point presentation should contain of 6-8 slides
  • PPT should cover the problem statement, proposed solution, technology employed, benefits, and the novelty of the project
    • Round-2(Finals) :
  • Selected teams are required to pitch their product,presenting with a prototype for a live demonstration.
  • Prototypes can range from low-fidelity options like diagrams and 2D representations to medium-fidelity choices such as videos, frameworks, and 3D models crafted from materials like clay or ice cream sticks.
    • Round-Details :
    Round 1 : Idea Submission We are open to all aspiring entrepreneurs from all colleges,participants can submit their groundbreaking ideas as an abstract.We're looking for projects that can make a positive impact,from sustainable solutions to tech innovations.Expert judges evaluate submissions based on creativity,feasibility and Novelty.The top teams will advance to the next round.
    Round 2 : Facing the Sharks The selected teams present their ideas lively to a group of experienced entrepreneurs,"the sharks".During the pitch,the participants will present their "product idea" with their prototype (low or medium fidelity) and what makes their product unique and valuable to the Sharks.The sharks evaluate and make investment offers to the ideas and the best product is selected.It is a high energy competition where each team's fate is at stake.
    Pre-requisite : Team Size : 1-4 Enterpreneur Skills
