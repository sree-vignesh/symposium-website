THE 60-SECOND SPOTLIGHT

Description : The 60-second spotlight is an engaging event that brings together creative minds to create and produce short video advertisements. Participants are tasked with crafting compelling narratives and visually captivating content within a limited time frame.

Detailed Description :

Participants work in a team, leveraging their diverse skills and expertise to ideate, script, film, and edit their advertisements. The event typically provides participants with a theme or a specific product or service to promote, challenging them to showcase their creativity and ingenuity in delivering impactful messages to their target audience.

  • Total hours- 3hrs
  • 1.5hr- video shooting
  • 1.5hr – editing
  • Advertisement time limit – 60secs - 3mins
    • Rules & Regulations :
  • Videos should be shot in smart phones and not in professional cameras.
  • Using AI apps for editing is strictly prohibited.
  • Editing should not be done during video shooting time and should be done during allocated editing time (under supervision).
  • The duration of the advertisement should be within 60secs to 3mins.
  • The videos can be edited on both laptops and smartphones.
  • Engaging in malpractice will result in team disqualification.
    • Pre-requisite : Team Size : 1-4 Editing Skills
