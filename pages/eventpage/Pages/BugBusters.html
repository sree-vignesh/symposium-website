BUG - BUSTERS

Description : BUG-BUSTERS is a dynamic and challenging code debugging event designed to test participants' programming prowess, problem-solving skills, and time management. This event aims to engage and foster teamwork among participants, allowing them to showcase their debugging abilities under time constraints.

Round Description:
Round 1: Preliminary Round - "Code Crunch":
  • Each team should contain 2 members.
  • The scheduled activity will span a duration of 45 minutes with the primary objective of assessing participants' initial debugging skills.
  • The plan involves distributing diverse code snippets to each team, who will be tasked with identifying and resolving as many issues as possible within the allocated time frame.
  • Success in this phase will determine the top-performing teams based on their adept debugging capabilities.
  • Those teams demonstrating a superior number of correct debugs will advance to the subsequent round of the competition.
    • Round 2: Finals - "Error Odyssey" :
  • In this final phase, the selected finalists will encounter a more challenging set of code snippets carefully designed to test both individual and collaborative debugging skills.
  • Teams are required to strategically approach each problem during rotations, aiming to maximize their efficiency in problem-solving.
  • The determining factor for declaring the BUG-BUSTERS champions will be the team with the highest number of correctly debugged problems in 60 min, with tiebreaker criteria applied if necessary.
    • Rules :
  • Team Composition: Participants must form teams of two, and only one member can debug at a time.
  • Team Rotation: Swap roles every 5 minutes to ensure fair distribution of responsibilities and effective communication.
  • Scoring Criteria: Teams will be evaluated based on the number of successfully debugged problems, with a tiebreaker favoring faster completion.
  • Debugging Environment: Participants receive pre-loaded code snippets with deliberate errors to debug within the specified time frame.
  • Communication: Teams can communicate only during the designated rotation period, with no communication allowed outside that timeframe.
  • Duration: The total duration of the event will be 2 hours 30 min.
