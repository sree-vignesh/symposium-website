DESIGN DYNAMO

Description : Design Dynamo is an exhilarating design competition using Figma. After receiving a prototype developed in Round 1, teams selected to 2nd round must swiftly adapt to a randomly assigned twist. The challenge demands lightning-speed creativity, adaptability, and design ingenuity.

Round 1- Prototype Development (online mode):

Teams will design and submit their UI Figma prototypes through the designated online platform within the specified deadline. The submitted prototypes will be evaluated and teams with mind-blowing prototype will be shortlisted to the second round.
Round 2-Mystery Roulette Challenge (offline mode):

  • Mystery Roulette Setup: Teams will face a Mystery Roulette wheel which will be set up. Each section of the wheel will contain a unique twist or challenge.

  • Twists and Challenges: Upon spinning the wheel, teams will be presented with a twist or challenge, ranging from unforeseen user scenarios, technological constraints, design constraints, or thematic variations.

  • Adaptation and Prototyping: Teams must quickly adapt to the given twist and modify their UI Figma prototypes from Round 1 to incorporate the new requirements or constraints within the stipulated time frame.

  • Presentation to Jury: Once the adaptation phase concludes, teams will present their updated prototypes to the jury panel.
    • Rules & Regulations :
  • Team will be select based on the round 1 (through online submission) the team can prepare the prototype based on their own theme.
  • Teams must use the prototype provided during Round 1 as a foundation for round 2.
  • The assigned twist from the "Mystery Roulette" must be integrated seamlessly into the prototype.
  • Presentations should highlight the adaptability to unexpected constraints.
  • Plagiarism is strictly prohibited.
  • The team will get 1 hour 15 minutes for the creating the randomly assigned twist.
  • The teams will get 4 minutes for presentation, and 3 minutes for question and answer session.

    • Duration: 3 hours
